About

I’m an Electrical & Computer Engineering student focused on building reliable systems at the intersection of hardware and firmware. My experience spans STM32-based embedded control (PWM, ADC, timers/interrupts), I²C/UART communication, and modular driver development. I also design and verify digital logic in VHDL (FSMs, datapaths, simulation/testbenches) and use MATLAB for modeling and data analysis.

I’m broadly interested in electrical engineering roles that emphasize strong fundamentals and real-world validation—embedded systems, hardware integration, test/verification, controls, and instrumentation—where clear architecture and measurement-driven debugging make the difference.

  • Embedded: STM32 microcontrollers, PWM control, ADC sensing, UART, I²C
  • Digital: VHDL/Verilog, FPGA design, finite state machines, simulation/testbenches
  • Analysis: MATLAB modeling + data analysis; systems & controls foundations
  • Tools: Git, gcc/make, gdb/valgrind; documentation and structured testing
